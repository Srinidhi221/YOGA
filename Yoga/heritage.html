Spiritual Significance

Yoga in Indian Philosophy

Yoga holds a central place in Hinduism and other Indian philosophical traditions. It serves as a bridge between the physical and spiritual realms, offering a path to self-realization and enlightenment. The practice of yoga is deeply intertwined with India's spiritual heritage, influencing various schools of thought and religious practices.

"Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self." - The Bhagavad Gita

Hinduism and Yoga

In Hinduism, yoga is considered one of the six orthodox schools of philosophy. It provides a systematic approach to spiritual growth, emphasizing the union of individual consciousness with universal consciousness.

Holistic Health Approach

Yoga's approach to health and well-being is comprehensive, addressing physical, mental, and spiritual aspects of human existence. This holistic approach has been practiced in India for thousands of years and continues to benefit millions worldwide.
